Frequently Asked Questions

What's the cutoff time for same-day delivery?

Orders must be placed before 12:00 PM for guaranteed same-day delivery within the metropolitan area.

What areas do you cover?

We offer same-day delivery in Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, and Da Nang. Next-day delivery is available nationwide.

What if no one is available to receive?

We'll attempt redelivery the next business day at no extra charge, or you can redirect to a neighbor or safe location.

Are there size or weight limits?

Same-day delivery supports packages up to 5kg. For larger items, our next-day service handles up to 30kg.
