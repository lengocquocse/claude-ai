Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum storage commitment?

We offer flexible terms starting from one month. Long-term contracts receive preferential rates and dedicated space allocation.

Can I access my inventory anytime?

Standard storage includes business hours access. Premium and Fulfillment plans include 24/7 facility access with advance notice.

How do you handle inventory discrepancies?

We conduct regular cycle counts and provide detailed discrepancy reports. Our accuracy rate exceeds 99.9% across all facilities.

Do you offer e-commerce integration?

Yes, we integrate with major platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, Lazada, and Shopee for automated order fulfillment.
