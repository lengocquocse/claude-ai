Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifies as a high-value shipment?

Items valued over 10 million VND, including electronics, jewelry, artwork, medical equipment, and important documents.

What temperature ranges do you support?

We offer frozen (-20C to -25C), refrigerated (2C to 8C), and controlled room temperature (15C to 25C) options.

How do I file an insurance claim?

Claims can be filed within 48 hours of delivery. Our dedicated claims team processes most requests within 5 business days.

Do you provide packaging materials?

Yes, we offer specialized packaging including foam inserts, thermal containers, and custom crating for artwork and antiques.
